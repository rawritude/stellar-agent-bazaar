# Event Writing

## Writing rules

- short
- vivid
- funny
- commercially meaningful
- never random just for randomness

## Event structure

Every event should include:
- setup
- decision or automatic result
- money effect
- reputation or information effect
- memorable line

## Example events

### The Cinnamon Panic
A district influencer claims cinnamon is the new status spice.
Demand surges for 24 hours.
If you have inventory, margins rise.
If your scout investigates, they may discover the rumor was started by a wholesaler cousin.

### Permit Goblin Strike
Clerks stop stamping market permits until someone funds the Official Morale Tea Tin.
Pay a small fee now or lose district access for the day.

### The Marquis Performs a Discount
Your brand ambassador gives a wildly compelling speech and accidentally promises impossible prices.
Sales rise. Margins collapse. Reputation becomes confusing.

### Counterfeit Apricot Emergency
A rival is accused of fake apricot concentrate.
You can stay quiet, investigate, or exploit the chaos with a premium honesty campaign.
